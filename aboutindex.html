About
The
Artist
Hi, my name is Solomon Drader. I'm a design student at Massey University. My work is about faces and places, but mostly faces. This collection of drawings are drawn
in Adobe Illustrator using a digital drawing tablet. Many are drawn eyes closed because drawing with my mind encapsulates my ideas more succesfully and succinctly than drawing with my eyes.
